RULES FOR BINGO GAME :

1. The game is played on a grid that's 4 circles by 4 circles.
2. The players take turns choosing the number from their card. When Player 1 plays the Player 2 card disappears and opposite.
3. The first player to get 4 numbers in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
4. If both players have 4 numbers in a row, the game ends in a tie.
